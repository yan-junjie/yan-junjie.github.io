Junjie Yan 闫俊杰

CTO of Smart City BG & Vice Head of Research

SenseTime Group Limited

Junjie Yan is the CTO of Smart City Business Group and Vice Head of Research at SenseTime. He leads the R&D Team within Smart City Group to build systems and algorithms that make cities safer and more efficient. At SenseTime Research, he leads the Deep Learning Toolchian Team to build deep learning toolchain from algorithm components to distributed training and inference platform that enables deep learning solutions scale up to more than 700 customers. His research interests include computer vision, deep learning and distributed systems.
